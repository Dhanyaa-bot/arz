Feeding Instruction:

Our Multigrains & Dry Fruits cereal blends a variety of multigrains with soft, nutritious dry fruits, offering a balanced mix of essential vitamins and minerals. This blend provides natural sweetness and is easy for babies to digest, making it a great choice for early feeding. The diverse flavors and textures ensure that your baby enjoys a satisfying meal while receiving a range of nutrients.

  • Start with one spoon of ARZ baby powder on the first day
    and gradually increase the amount.
  • Consult your pediatrician before introducing
    any new food to your child.
  • Use prepared feed within half an hour;
    discard any leftovers.

Cooking Directions:

EASY TO COOK AND EASY DIGESTION
  • Wash your hands and make sure
    all utensils are clean
  • Add 80 ml of water (can use milk above 1 year) to 2 tbs of ARZ Baby powder and mix lump-free
  • Cook the mixture on a medium flame for
    3 to 5 min, keep stirring. continuously.
  • Adjust the porridge consistency as
    desired and Serve it warm
  • Optionally add any to enhance the taste
    Ghee | Dates Powder Any fruits or Vegetable puree. Curd (above 1yr) | Honey (above 1yr)
